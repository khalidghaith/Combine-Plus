Effortless Organization

Importing content shouldn't be a chore. With Combine+, you can:

  • Drag & Drop files directly from Explorer.
  • Paste Screenshots directly from your clipboard.
  • Smart Selection with orange active states.

Intuitive Rearranging

Order matters. Drag items to their perfect position using the visual insertion markers, or throw them to the "Drop Tail" to send them to the end of the line instantly.

"Look for the orange insertion markers that indicate exactly where your file will land."

Built-in Previewer

Stop opening external apps just to check a page. Double-click any item to launch the integrated viewer.

  • Zoom in for details
  • Flip through pages instantly
